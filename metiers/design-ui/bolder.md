Identify areas where the design plays it too safe -- generic layouts, timid color choices, uniform sizing, and predictable patterns. Increase visual contrast by varying scale, introducing bolder type weights, adding color accents, or breaking symmetry where it creates energy. Push the design toward a stronger visual identity while maintaining usability and readability. The goal is to make the interface memorable and distinctive, not just functional.
