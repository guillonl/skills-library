Find opportunities to inject personality and joy into functional moments -- success states, loading sequences, empty states, and transitions. Add micro-interactions that reward user actions, playful copy that breaks monotony, and subtle animations that make the interface feel alive. Delight should feel earned and natural, never forced or distracting. Focus on the moments users encounter most and the moments that matter most emotionally.
