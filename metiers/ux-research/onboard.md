Audit the first-run experience from a new user's perspective -- what do they see, what do they understand, and how quickly do they reach value. Design progressive onboarding that teaches through doing rather than reading. Create compelling empty states that guide action instead of showing blank screens. Implement contextual tooltips, smart defaults, and sample data that demonstrate the product's value immediately. Every step should reduce time-to-value and build user confidence without overwhelming them.
