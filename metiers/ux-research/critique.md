Evaluate the interface through multiple UX lenses -- visual hierarchy, information architecture, cognitive load, emotional resonance, and task flow efficiency. Score each dimension quantitatively and walk through the experience as distinct user personas (novice, power user, accessibility-dependent user). Identify friction points, confusing patterns, and missed opportunities. Deliver actionable recommendations ranked by user impact, with specific before/after suggestions for each issue.
